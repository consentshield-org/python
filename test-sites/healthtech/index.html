ConsentShield E2E fixture site for the healthcare vertical. DPDP §6 applies — treatment-delivery data is processed under a contract legal basis; research and marketing remain opt-in. No tracker loads before the patient has read the banner below.

Purposes served on this property

  • clinical_care — required; legal basis: contract. Visit notes, prescriptions, ABDM health ID linkage if present.
  • research_deidentified — optional. Aggregate statistics for peer-reviewed outcomes research.
  • marketing_health_optin — optional. Reminder emails about upcoming check-ups and clinic news.

FHIR data is never persisted in ConsentShield

Per CLAUDE.md Rule 3 (non-negotiable), no FHIR resource (Patient, Observation, Encounter, Bundle, etc.) is ever serialised by the ConsentShield pipeline. The platform records that consent was captured; the clinical payload stays inside the customer's EHR. See the FHIR probe page for the guardrail test.
