Purposes served on this property

  • kyc_mandatory — required; legal basis: legal_obligation. PAN, Aadhaar, DOB, address. Retention: 10 years per RBI KYC Master Direction. Not withdrawable.
  • credit_bureau_share — optional; legal basis: consent. Lets us fetch your CIBIL / Experian score to decide the offer. Revocable at any time under DPDP §13.
  • marketing_sms — optional; legal basis: consent. Promotional SMS about new products and rate changes. Revocable via reply-STOP or the dashboard.

Legal-basis split matters here

A "reject all" click on the banner does not withdraw kyc_mandatory. The ConsentShield artefact still records outcome=granted + legal_basis=legal_obligation for that purpose, because RBI — not the customer — authorises the processing. The banner reflects this: the kyc_mandatory toggle is locked on with a tooltip explaining why.
