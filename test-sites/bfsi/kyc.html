Who are you?

RBI Master Direction on KYC (2016) requires this data before any NBFC can extend credit. Collected under kyc_mandatory — legal basis: legal_obligation.

Masked per UIDAI guidance. Full Aadhaar OTP flow in production.

What happens to this data?

  • Name + PAN + DOB + address — retained by NeoFin for 10 years per RBI KYC MD.
  • Aadhaar last 4 — stored masked; full-number capture happens via UIDAI OTP flow (not in this demo).
  • Income declaration — used only for underwriting; retained for the tenor of any loan + 8 years post-closure per RBI §45ZC of BR Act.

None of these can be deleted via a DPDP §13 right-to-erasure request while the legal obligation is in force. The ConsentShield retention engine (ADR-1004) honours this by recording a retention_suppression with citation when such a revocation lands.
