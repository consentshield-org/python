Why kyc_mandatory can't be turned off here

Under DPDP §7(a), an organisation may process personal data without explicit consent when doing so is necessary to comply with a law. RBI's KYC Master Direction is such a law for NBFCs. The consent artefact is still emitted (with outcome=granted) so the audit record is complete; the legal_basis field is legal_obligation rather than consent, and the withdrawable flag is false. Any DPDP §13 right-to-erasure request against these rows returns a 409 Conflict until the obligation lapses.
